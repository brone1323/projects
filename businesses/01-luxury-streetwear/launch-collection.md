# Launch Collection — Luxury Streetwear

## Objective

Create a tight premium capsule collection that can launch quickly and sell real products without inventory.

## Collection Rules

- 10 core product types.
- Multiple sizes per product.
- Limited color palette.
- No oversized catalog.
- Minimal branding.
- Premium blanks only.
- Strong product photography direction.

## Proposed Product Types

1. Heavyweight oversized T-shirt
2. Classic premium T-shirt
3. Heavyweight hoodie
4. Crewneck sweatshirt
5. Sweatpants
6. Shorts
7. Zip hoodie
8. Long sleeve tee
9. Embroidered cap
10. Beanie or tote bag

## Preferred Color Palette

- Black
- Vintage black
- White
- Cream
- Stone
- Heather grey
- Olive
- Brown
- Navy

## Product Requirements

Each product should include:

- Supplier product ID.
- Blank brand.
- Fit.
- Fabric weight.
- Material composition.
- Available sizes.
- Available colors.
- Decoration method.
- Branding options.
- Supplier cost.
- Shipping estimate.
- Retail price.
- Gross margin estimate.

## Pricing Position

The brand should be priced as elevated basics, not cheap merch.

Indicative pricing targets:

- T-shirts: $45–75
- Hoodies: $95–150
- Crewnecks: $85–130
- Sweatpants: $85–130
- Caps: $35–60

Final pricing must be based on actual supplier costs and fulfillment fees.
