# Business 01 — Luxury Streetwear

## Status

Approved for deeper implementation planning.

## Business Thesis

Build a premium streetwear ecommerce brand using a custom website and API-driven fulfillment. The business should be able to sell real products quickly while also becoming a high-end ecommerce portfolio piece.

## Constraints

- No inventory purchase.
- No MOQ.
- No supplier financial commitment.
- No generic Shopify storefront.
- Supplier must support API-driven fulfillment or be technically workable through an integration layer.
- Customer-facing experience must be fully branded.
- Supplier should remain replaceable.

## Initial Supplier Direction

Primary candidate: Apliiq.

Reason: Apliiq is positioned toward private-label streetwear and supports custom branding, white-label fulfillment, and developer/API workflows.

## Positioning

Elevated basics / minimal luxury streetwear.

Avoid:
- Cheap POD look.
- Meme apparel.
- Generic merch.
- Large unfocused catalogs.

Prefer:
- Heavyweight garments.
- Oversized fits.
- Minimal branding.
- Collection-based drops.
- Editorial presentation.

## Launch Goal

Launch with approximately 10 products with size and color options, capable of taking real orders quickly.
